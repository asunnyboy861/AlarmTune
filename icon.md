# AlarmTune App Icon - OpenAI Image Generation Prompts

## Prompt 1 (Recommended - Primary)
A minimalist iOS app icon for an alarm clock app called "AlarmTune" that features independent volume control. The icon shows a sleek alarm clock silhouette with a prominent volume slider wave emanating from the speaker, symbolizing custom sound levels. The design uses a smooth gradient from deep navy blue at the top to vibrant electric blue at the bottom. The clock face is simple and modern with no numbers, just two clean hands pointing to 7:00. Three ascending sound wave arcs flow from the clock's base, representing adjustable volume. The style is flat, bold, and Apple-inspired with clean geometric shapes. No text, no rounded corners, no shadows. Pure white clock elements on the blue gradient background. The icon must be instantly recognizable at small sizes.

## Prompt 2 (Alternative - Sound Wave Focus)
A clean modern iOS app icon featuring a stylized sound wave that morphs into an alarm bell shape. The design uses a warm gradient from rich purple to bright cyan blue. The central element is a bold, simplified bell icon where the clapper is replaced by a horizontal volume slider with a circular thumb. The bell has smooth rounded edges and the volume slider extends slightly beyond the bell's width. The overall composition is centered and symmetrical. Flat design, no 3D effects, no text, no shadows. The icon should feel premium, calming, and tech-forward. White icon elements on the purple-to-cyan gradient. Apple App Store style, works at 60x60 pixels.

## Prompt 3 (Alternative - Minimal Volume)
A super minimal iOS app icon for a volume-control alarm clock app. The design features a single bold circle representing a clock face, with a prominent horizontal volume slider bar cutting across the lower third of the circle. The slider thumb is a bright accent dot. The background is a clean gradient from dark teal to bright mint green. The clock hands are simple white lines. The volume bar is white with a bright yellow-green accent thumb. Ultra-clean, flat design with maximum simplicity. No numbers, no text, no extra details. The icon must convey "alarm + volume" at a glance. Apple design language, bold shapes, high contrast, works perfectly at small thumbnail sizes.

## Design Notes
- All prompts follow Apple HIG 2025 guidelines: simplicity, bold shapes, gradient backgrounds
- No text in icons (Apple rejects text-heavy icons)
- No pre-applied rounded corners (system applies them)
- No transparency (must be solid PNG 1024x1024)
- Primary color palette: blue/navy (trust, calm, technology)
- Accent colors: cyan, mint, yellow-green (energy, alertness)
- The "volume slider" element is the key differentiator from generic alarm clock icons
